Our focus

Reverse Osmosis Systems (Brackish & Sea Water)

Permatech offers a complete range of custom compact, skid mounted water treatment reverse osmosis plants.

Water Treatment Components

Permatech, in partnership with world-leading manufacturers, offers wide array of commercial water components, spare parts and accessories such as pumps (i.e Grundfos), membrane filters, membranes (i.e TORAY), filters, fittings, RO components, booster pumps (i.e Grundfos), storage tanks, installation accessories and the latest state-of-art instruments for laboratory use and on-site analysis.

Build, Operate and Transport (BOT)

BOT is a type of contract which combines various types of expertise: design-build, services and financing arrangements. Permatech, represented by a company dedicated to the project, provides the design, construction, financing and operation of the water treatment plant. In return, the customer undertakes to pay for the service provided for them according to a rate covering both the plant operating activity and also the amortisation of the capital invested. The plant is transferred to the customer at the end of the contract, which generally lasts 20 to 30 years. The latter, of course, has the benefit of the technical expertise and know-how provided by Permatech throughout the contract.

Membrane Cleaning & Pretreatment Chemicals

RO systems creates salt concentrated water streams that could produce scale or fouling, while “the narrow feedwater channel found in your spiral wound and hollow fiber elements accentuates scaling and fouling”. Permatech’s (PT3000 Series) membrane anti-scalants are a pretreatment programs designed to eliminate scale and reduce fouling in membrane systems, regardless of the feedwater source.We offer technical expertise and assistance in design, troubleshooting and cleaning of RO membranes to give you an optimum system performance.
