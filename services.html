About Permatech

We strive to deliver the upmost quality of products and services

Founded in 2013, Permatech is a company that provides sustainable water solutions to its clients. Permatech strives to deliver the upmost quality of products that ranges from Reverse Osmosis Membranes, ultra filters, pressure vessels and other related water treatment products. Permatech main objective is to maintain the client’s design flux rates, minimize the membranes cleaning frequency, and prolong the useful life of the RO equipment and components.

Mission

We are dedicated to bringing the most valuable solutions and providing the best customer experience in meeting water and environmental needs through our products and services.

Vision

We are working towards becoming the most respected and valued water treatment company in the MENA region; in the eyes of our customers, employees, stakeholders as well as the communities within which we operate.

Value

We are committed to meeting and exceeding our customer's expectations by continuously improving our knowledge platform.

Sustainability Initiatives

We are committed to providing environmentally responsible and economically viable water treatment solutions that conserve energy and water while protecting the health and welfare of people and the environment.
