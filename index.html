About us

Permatech

Founded in 2013, Permatech is a company that provides sustainable water solutions to its clients. Permatech strives to deliver the upmost quality of products that ranges from Reverse Osmosis Membranes, ultra filters, pressure vessels and other related water treatment products. Permatech main objective is to maintain the client’s design flux rates, minimize the membranes cleaning frequency, and prolong the useful life of the RO equipment and components.

Our services

Preliminary & Detailed Water Treatment Design

Our Team of experts has a proven reputation for high quality water treatment plant-design projects, including:

  • Process Engineering and design for water treatment systems
  • Process Engineering and design for water treatment systems
  • Technical, economic and financial feasibility studies
  • Existing water treatment plant upgrades
  • QA/QC & value engineering

Installation, Maintenance & Commissioning

Our service specialists undergo in-depth training to ensure that they can troubleshoot and fix any issue that may arise on site. Given our extensive experience, we install, commission and maintain your water treatment plant in the most efficient way possible.

RO Membranes off-site Cleaning & Restoration

This service is available when on-site cleaning is not practical or the client has inadequate clean-in-place (CIP) systems. The RO membranes are pre-tested, cleaned and post-tested with the goal of restoring performance to original specifications. This ensures improved permeate flow & rejection and reduces delta pressure to give an overall optimum plant operation.
