Compact Water (RO) Filtration System - 5 Stages

Description:

A Molecular Water Filtration System based on reverse osmosis with a prefiltration, RO membrane closed in housing and postfiltration installed in a brand new compact, ergonomic casing designed especially for modern kitchens. Prefiltration is based on cartridges that possess an increased filtration capacity with Quick-Disconnect design that makes replacement easy and without the necessity to use any tools. Molecular filtration technology filters the source water up to 0,0001 micron and removes e.g. heavy metals, radioactive and carcinogenic elements. Usage of mineralization technology reintroduces beneficial minerals into water.
