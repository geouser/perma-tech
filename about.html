About Permatech

We strive to deliver the upmost quality of products and services

Founded in 2013, Permatech is a company that provides sustainable water solutions to its clients. Permatech strives to deliver the upmost quality of products that ranges from Reverse Osmosis Membranes, ultra filters, pressure vessels and other related water treatment products. Permatech main objective is to maintain the client’s design flux rates, minimize the membranes cleaning frequency, and prolong the useful life of the RO equipment and components.
